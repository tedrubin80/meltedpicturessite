Frequently Asked

What AI tools do you use?

We utilize a combination of cutting-edge generative AI models including video synthesis, image generation, and audio processing tools. Our workflow combines multiple AI systems to achieve our distinct visual style.

Do you accept script submissions?

We're always interested in hearing from fellow horror enthusiasts with unique concepts that could benefit from AI-generated visuals. Send your pitch to hello@meltedpictures.com.

Are your films suitable for all audiences?

Our films are primarily horror and thriller content intended for mature audiences. Individual film pages include content warnings where appropriate.

Can I license your films?

For licensing inquiries, please contact us directly at hello@meltedpictures.com with details about your intended use.
