About Us

Where artificial intelligence meets the art of terror

Our Vision

Melted Pictures was founded on a singular vision: to explore the uncanny intersection of artificial intelligence and horror cinema. We believe that AI doesn't just replicate human creativity—it reveals new dimensions of the unsettling and unknown.

Every frame of our films is generated through advanced AI systems, creating visuals and narratives that feel both eerily familiar and disturbingly alien. This is not just filmmaking; it's an exploration of what happens when machines dream our nightmares.

The Philosophy

Horror has always been about confronting the unknown. AI introduces a new kind of unknown—images and stories that emerge from vast neural networks, trained on humanity's collective imagination yet producing something distinctly inhuman.

We embrace the glitches, the uncanny valleys, the moments where the AI produces something that shouldn't exist. These imperfections become features, enhancing the unsettling atmosphere that defines our work.
